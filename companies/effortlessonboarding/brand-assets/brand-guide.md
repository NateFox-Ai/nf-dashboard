## Typography

### Headlines
- **Font:** System font stack (-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto)
- **Weight:** 700-800 (bold/extrabold)
- **Sizes:** 
  - H1: 42px (desktop), 32px (mobile)
  - H2: 28px
  - H3: 20px

### Body Text
- **Font:** Same system stack
- **Weight:** 400 (normal)
- **Size:** 16px base
- **Line height:** 1.6

### Accent Text
- **Color:** `#64748b` (muted gray)
- **Usage:** Descriptions, meta text

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Professional but approachable** — Expert without being stuffy
- **Action-oriented** — Focus on results and outcomes
- **Empathetic** — We understand the pain of manual work
- **Confident** — We know automation works

### Tone Guidelines
| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Website | Professional, benefit-focused |
| Sales emails | Enthusiastic, problem-solving |
| Support | Patient, helpful, clear |
| Social media | Conversational, behind-the-scenes |

### Words to Use
- Effortless, seamless, automated
- Save time, scale, streamline
- Custom, tailored, built for you
- Results, ROI, hours saved

### Words to Avoid
- Cheap, easy (implies low value)
- Magic (implies we don't know how it works)
- Guaranteed (legal risk)
- Cheap, discount (positions us as low-cost)

## Messaging Framework

### For Coaches
"Stop losing clients to messy onboarding. We build custom automations that handle your entire client journey — from inquiry to signed contract — while you focus on coaching."

### For Consultants
"Your expertise shouldn't be buried in admin work. We automate your client onboarding so you can deliver more value and scale your practice."

### Key Value Props
1. **Time Saved:** 10+ hours per week
2. **Consistency:** Every client gets the same premium experience
3. **Scalability:** Handle more clients without more work
4. **Professionalism:** Look like a bigger operation
