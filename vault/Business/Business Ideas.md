# Business Ideas Research

*Research date: 2026-04-21*
*Constraints: $100 budget, 90 days, $20k goal*

---

## 🥇 Option 1: AI Automation Agency (AAA)

**What:** Build custom workflow automations for SMBs using n8n/Make.com

**Startup Cost:** $0-50
- n8n: free (self-hosted)
- Domain: ~$12/year
- Basic tools: optional

**Pricing:**
- Projects: $500-5,000
- Hourly: $100-300/hour
- Maintenance: $100-500/month retainers

**Market:** SMBs drowning in manual tasks (data entry, lead gen, reporting, integrations)

**Pros:**
- High demand right now
- Immediate cash flow
- Recurring maintenance revenue
- I can code custom integrations (not just no-code)

**Cons:**
- Trading time for money initially
- Client management overhead

**Validation:** Someone made $4,200 in 4 months part-time selling n8n workflows ($50-150 per workflow)

**My Edge:** Can build complex custom integrations beyond drag-and-drop

## 🥈 Option 2: AI Content Editing & Fact-Checking Service

**What:** Human review of AI-generated content for accuracy, tone, SEO

**Startup Cost:** $0-30
- Just need website/domain

**Pricing:**
- Per word: $0.05-0.15
- Per article: $50-200
- Retainers: $500-2,000/month

**Market:** Content agencies, publishers, businesses using ChatGPT for blogs

**Pros:**
- Zero startup cost
- AI content is everywhere and full of errors
- Scalable with contractors later

**Cons:**
- Commoditized service
- Price competition from overseas

**Validation:** Fiverr has active gigs; Originality.ai charges $12.95/mo just for AI detection

## 🥉 Option 3: Micro-SaaS (Niche Workflow Tool)

**What:** Small tool solving one specific pain point

**Examples:**
- TikTok scheduling for dentists
- Niche CRM for specific industry
- URL shortener with special features

**Startup Cost:** $20-80
- Hosting: ~$5-20/month
- Domain: ~$12/year
- Template (optional): ~$50

**Pricing:** $10-50/month per user

**Math for $20k:**
- At $25/month: need ~800 users
- At $50/month: need ~400 users

**Pros:**
- Recurring revenue
- Scalable
- Can sell for 3-5x ARR later

**Cons:**
- Slower to first dollar
- Need to find validated niche
- Marketing challenge

**Validation:** Multiple solo founders hitting $10k+ MRR with simple tools

**What:** Templates, guides, courses on automation/AI

**Examples:**

**Cons:**

## Option 5: Niche Newsletter + Sponsorships

**What:** Curated AI/automation news for specific industry

**Startup Cost:** $0-20
- Beehiiv/Substack: free tiers
- Domain: optional

**Pricing:** $20-50 CPM on sponsorships

**Math for $20k:**
- Need ~10k subscribers with consistent opens

**Pros:**
- Builds audience asset
- Multiple monetization paths
- Long-term value

**Cons:**
- Slower to revenue
- 90 days is tight for this model

## 🎯 Recommended Strategy

**Phase 1 (Days 1-30):** AI Automation Agency
- Fast cash flow
- Learn what people actually need
- Build case studies

**Phase 2 (Days 30-90):** Use agency profits to fund Micro-SaaS
- Build tool based on agency client needs
- Agency clients become SaaS beta users
- Two revenue streams
